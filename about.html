About Us

The purpose of the website is to help the patient and the doctor to know everything related to medicines, including symptoms, active substances and other similar medicines, and when to take them as well. The patient can also, through the website, know what medication is suitable for him by diagnosing him by stating his symptoms, age, gender, and whether he suffers from chronic diseases such as heart pressure, diabetes, etc....
